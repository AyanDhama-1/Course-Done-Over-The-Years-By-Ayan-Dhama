City Palace,Udaipur
City Palace in Udaipur is a stunning complex of palaces situated on the banks of Lake Pichola.Built over nearly 400 years, it showcases a blend of Mughal and Rajasthani architectural styles.

The palace complex includes museums, courtyards, and gardens, making it a major tourist attraction.Its intricate peacock mosaics and panoramic views of the city and lake are especially renowned.
